Buffal Chicken Dip

Description

This buffalo chicken dip is easy and is perfect for the big game. This recipe really brings new meaning to the saying "set it and forget it" No worrying about turning on the oven or cooking ingredients seperately, just add everything to the crock pot and voila, deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 2 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese
  • 4 cups shredded cooked chicken (rotisserie works well here)
  • 1 cup hot sauce
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions/scallions
  • blue cheese crumbles

Steps

  1. Pleace cream cheese, chicken, hot sauce, ranch dressing, and 1/2 cup of the cheddar cheese. Cover
  2. Cook on high 1 1/2 -2 hours or until heated throughout. Stir until well-mixed, top with remaining 1/2 cup cheese and sprinkle with green onion. Cover. Cook additional 15 minutes or until cheese is melted. Serve sprinkled with blue cheese.
