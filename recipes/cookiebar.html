Chocolate Chip Cookie Bar

Description

This peanut butter chocolate chip cookie bar will absolutely knock your socks off. This is a delicious recipe that has been in my family for generations. If you want impress everyone at the party then bring out this delicious desert.

This recipe is very easy and will be a hit no matter where you decide to bring it!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, or more to taste
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 3/4 cups unbleached flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 10 oz chocolate chunks

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together with an electric mixer in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat egg and vanilla extract into the butter mixture. Add peanut butter and beat until incorporated
  3. Silt flour and baking soda together in a seperate bowl; mix into the butter mixture. Fold chocolate chunks into the mixture.
  4. Spread the batter into a 13x9 - inch baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20-25 minutes. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
